Hanakotoba (Language of Flowers)

The purpose of this project was to design something that tackles major issues within Sydney. The issues being addressed by this project include: Inefficient consumption of energy from office buildings, pollution, sustainability and water conservation. The implementation of several of these structures throughout Sydney would prove to be highly beneficial to not only Australiaâ€™s economy and energy efficiency of both commercial and residential groups but also to the environment. This is due to its ability to not consume energy from the grid and at the same time also generate a large reserve of renewable solar energy which can be fed back into the grid. Furthermore, the water tanks reservoir is large enough to aptly provide water to the employees through the buildings facilities and also discharge water through drip irrigation to the green wall, ensuring it remains healthy.
