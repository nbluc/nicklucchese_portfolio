UNSW Light Rail Stop

UNSW Light Rail Stop

The light rail stop allows for efficient transportation of people by car, bus, light rail, foot and bicycle. This design also keeps interference between pedestrians and any form of vehicle to a minimum by redirecting traffic underneath a footbridge briefly.

An aerial view of the light rail stop and its facade.

This image showcases the underground aspect of the lightrail stop. To keep accidents and interference to a minimum, vehicles such as cars and trucks are redirected underneath a footbridge briefly. Allowing students above to cross at leisure whilst also prohibiting large groups of people to form whilst waiting for lights.
