Barangaroo Technology Analysis

Barangaroo Technology Analysis

The purpose of this project was to observe the interactions between people and technology. Most people who were recorded at Barangaroo Reserve were engaging with technology however not subversively that is, it does not undermine or ‘destroy’ the existing physical environment as it was not the intended purpose of those social groups. Technology was not central to the interactions that occurred throughout the precinct, it merely enhanced the lone person’s experience when undertaking recreational activities.
