An Overview

A majority of the work in this portfolio is modelled around the concepts of Sustainability and Self-sufficiency. With the implementation and intended use of advanced technologies and innovative engineering, we can develop an urban environment which has the potential to reduce its carbon foot print to zero. Through detailed surveying of suburbs, the construction and development of capable edifices can be seamlessly integrated into todays society.

Projects

This is a collection of my undergraduate works to date. These projects have been selected due to their relevance to the concepts of sustainability and self-sufficient constructs which, utilise various methods to reduce carbon emissions and the consumption of resources such as energy and water.
