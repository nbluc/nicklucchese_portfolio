Kurēn House (House of Cranes)

Kurēn House (House of Cranes)

The residence was originally designed for a client, in this case, it was for a film writer. Spanning over a single 120 square metre block of land and consisting of two floors the residence contains a master bedroom and ensuite bathroom, guest bedroom, bathroom, extensive open planned kitchen, living and dining areas, a pantry, laundry, writing space and outdoor and central gardens.

The central garden is encased by large, rounded glass panes which slide open behind the water feature, to allow for a seamless transition into an open-air tranquil environment within the comfort of the residents own home.

The private garden on the second floor acts as a space in which the client can interact with to relax and approach his task open minded within a more serene and inspiring area.
