Mech-Hana (Mechanical Flower)

Mech-Hana (Mechanical Flower)

Section of 1st floor. The black weaved like mesh coating the interior walls act as intricate sound barriers absorbing outside interference whilst also boosting acoustics inside the cinema.

The flower acts as both solar panels and a water catchment system. The slanted 'petals' guide the water into the center where beneath a water feature disguised as a water tank system sits.
